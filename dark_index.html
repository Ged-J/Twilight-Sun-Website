Project Description

"Twilight Sun" is a captivating RPG Roguelike game set in a mystical 2D world. The protagonist, Ilivan, an aging wizard, embarks on a quest to prevent a looming apocalypse by retrieving ancient artefacts. The game's world is procedurally generated, offering a fresh experience in each playthrough. It boasts advanced AI, a dynamic difficulty system, and a unique puzzle system, enhancing both the challenge and immersion. Players navigate through varied terrains, including mysterious mazes and dark dungeons, where strategic decision-making influences the game's narrative.

"Twilight Sun" offers an intriguing mix of exploration, strategy, and intense combat, set against a backdrop of high-fantasy aesthetics. This game promises to captivate players with its diverse environments, and engaging gameplay mechanics.
